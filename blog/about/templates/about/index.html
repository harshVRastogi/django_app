About Me

I am an android application developer for Navigators Software based in Kolkata. Between 2015 and 2016 I worked at Clef Software (Bengaluru) as a Jr. android application developer. I am fond of mobile applications but when it comes to android it's the easiest way of experiencing it.

I believe in doing something right at first at the cost of time. It's important for those who make robust applications. Take some code from others. Move it around, play with it, absorb & synthesize. Add structure, reinforcing the good parts and ripping out the bad bits. Patch up the holes that are left. Give it lyricism and grace and elegance. Make it for users, shaped so that they can feel the outcome, the way you feel it. That's software development. That's writing code.
